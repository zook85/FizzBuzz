FizzBuzz!

This is the FizzBuzz game. In this game you will have to count from 1 to 100 but with a twist! If the number is devisable by 3, you say "Fizz". If the number is devisable by 5, you say "Buzz". If the number is devisable by both, you say "FizzBuzz"

You will have to click what comes next below, you have 5 seconds for each answer. Can you get to 100?

The game starts when you give your first answer.
